# About

Hi, I’m **Ankul Choudhry**.

I’m a Senior Engineering Manager with over **14 years of experience** building and scaling backend and platform systems in high-traffic and regulated environments. Over the last several years, I’ve focused on leading teams that build reliable platforms while balancing product velocity, operational rigor, and long-term system health.

My background spans **fintech, insurance, and e-commerce**, where I’ve worked on systems handling **25K–30K+ RPM**, real-money transactions, and strict regulatory requirements.

---

## What I Do

I lead engineering teams that build and operate **mission-critical backend platforms**.

My work typically involves:
- Shaping technical direction and system architecture for high-scale services
- Partnering closely with Product, Finance, and Compliance teams
- Driving execution with clear ownership and predictable delivery
- Mentoring engineers and building strong, accountable teams

---

## Experience Highlights

At **Acko**, I lead multiple teams responsible for embedded insurance platforms across lending, travel, and commerce partners. My work has included:
- Delivering multi-category platforms contributing to **₹200+ Cr annualized portfolio impact**
- Designing regulatory-compliant, backward-compatible architectures
- Leading zero-downtime migrations of high-throughput systems
- Building internal platforms that reduced manual effort and turnaround time
- Introducing **AI-assisted development workflows** and document intelligence to improve delivery speed and operational accuracy

Earlier at **Snapdeal**, I worked on large-scale **checkout, payments, and order management systems**, including closed-wallet flows for instant payments and refunds, reconciliation systems, and secure payment integrations.
